Accounting Services

At BA Birojs we provide finance and accounting services for private and legal persons in Latvia. We work with self-employed persons, individual businessmen and small and medium limited liability companies. To our customers we offer a choice of long-term contracts with a monthly fee, short-term contracts for a determined period of time, or contracts for performance of certain tasks.

We also offer consultations in book-keeping, tax legislation and document circulation.

Senior accountant Biruta Aunina has 27 years of experience in bookkeeping. She has held the position of senior accountant for 21 years, 12 of which she has worked for international companies - SIA Dadsons and SIA Blue Coat Systems Latvia. She holds the certificate of the International Association of Book-keepers.

Languages: Latvian, Russian, English
